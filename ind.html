DASTAAN
Recreating the stories
Dastaan is a national college fest where technology and storytelling merge, focusing on societal transformation, environmental care, and innovation. "Awakening the Eternal Now" promises an impactful celebration of creativity and positive change. Engage in workshops, compete in thought-provoking events, and connect with industry leaders. Dastaan offers high visibility across 16 academic disciplines, networking opportunities, and brand enhancement. Join us in shaping the future at this vibrant festival that unites diverse minds and inspires new possibilities.
